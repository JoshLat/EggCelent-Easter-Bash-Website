Park Info

Address: 9501 Sashabaw Rd, Village of Clarkston, MI 48348

Independence Oaks Park is located in Oakland County and is typically open from 7 AM to 9 PM. It is the largest park in Oakland County at 1,286 acres with over 12 miles of trails. The parks activities range from baseball, biking, camping, hiking, rowing, ice skating, and cross country skating. Cost of an annual pass is no more than $30 for residents and $48 for non-residents.
